Our CTE Courses

Introduction to Engineering Design

In this course, students learn about the design process and its application. Students apply engineering standards and document their work during this course. Students also learn industry standard 3D modeling software.

Computer Integrated Manufacturing

In this course, students learn the life cycle of many common products. After learning about the manufacturing process, students design and create a model factory using these principles.

Digital Electronics

In this course, students learn about the inner workings of many popular electronic devices. Students learn soldering, logic design, engineering standards, and documentation.

Introduction to Aerospace Engineering

In this course, students learn the engineering disciplines involved in aerospace. Students design, build, and tweak airplane models, using industry-standard modeling software.

Project Management

In this course, students learn how to effectively lead, develop, and carry out projects. Students learn fundimental tasks of leadership, and will develop leadership skills through this course.

Engineering Design and Development

In this capstone course, students design and develop a solution to a real-world problem. Students work with mentors to collect information and deploy a solution to a complex real-world problem.
