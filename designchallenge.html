How we got here

Påskeæg is an all-new company, and we are based in Copenhagen, Denmark. We were founded after our founder's son was killed in a tragic car accident. She decided to dedicate her life to designing better, safer automated vehicles. We are now a global company, providing cutting-edge technology and developing safe and efficient autonomous cars at the forefront of the industry.
 
 
⬢ Partnership with Intel
In 2015, we partnered with Intel to release a special chip, built from the ground up to ensure live training of A.I. as well as instant responsiveness to multiple sources of data.
 
 
⬢ Data Collection
In early 2016, we launched a program to collect hundereds of millions of data points across many different types of vehicles, road conditions, and traffic situations. This data allows us to perform advanced analysis to create the safest, most intelligent self-driving car possible.
 
 
⬢ A.I. Training
In late 2018, we leveraged the power of our in-house cloud processing servers to feed these data points into the largest A.I. neural network in the world. This allows us to overlay the real-world data we collected onto any situation.
