How we got here

Påskeæg is an all new company based in Copenhagen, Denmark. We were founded when Thea Pallesen’s son was killed by a car. she decided to devote her life to automated vehicles. Now, we are a global company, providing cutting edge technology and developing safe and efficient autonomous cars.
 
 
⬢ Partnership with Intel
In 2015, we partnered with Intel to release a special chip, built from the ground up to ensure live training of A.I. as well as instant responsiveness to multiple sources of data.
